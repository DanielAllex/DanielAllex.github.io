AP Sourcebook

Through this project, I found 7 technology-related articles, most of which were researched as a previous research presentation, and looked for argument and rhetorical appeals. Through digitally annotating and then writing about each article, I was able to learn that even technology writers, who are thought to be all factual, apply strategies to better convey their arguments and usually hold some sort of argument even when first appearing informative rather than argumentative.
