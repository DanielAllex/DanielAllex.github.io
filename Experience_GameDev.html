Game Development

Ro-Volution is an online evolution game for PC, tablet, and phone. Players fight and scavenge for fruits in an attempt to become the largest player by the end of the round. As lead developer, I oversaw all aspects of development, but my primary focus was on item saving, game security, game mechanics, loot boxes, and all of the UI.
