Autonomous Vehicles: A Technology with Many Problems and Few Solutions (Research Paper)

Some time after I first did my research project on autonomous vehicles, I wanted to look into the topic more in depth. This time, instead of putting a lot of focus into how they actually work, I put more time into looking into how the technology opens up new problems. A lot of the essay involves outlining the problems with these vehicles, but I later use those problems to better explain how a solution can be viable. This was also the first full research paper that I used APA for instead of MLA because APA will be more used later on for a career in technology.
