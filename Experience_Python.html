Python Classes

Over the span of 4 days, a few other students and I taught Python to middle school students under the Monroe Township Public Library. It was originally meant to be in person, but due to the pandemic, we moved to Youtube livestreams. The same middle school students were able to watch the lessons live. The videos were after posted as segments for each topic for anyone that would later want to learn from them. Below is the playlist of the later cutdown lessons. I speak in the Strings, Integers and Floats, Booleans, and Casting segments.
